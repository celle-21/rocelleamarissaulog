ABOUT US


Late Nights & Coffee is a cafe established in 2020, with store locations in both the Philippines and Japan. The cafe was initially a small bakery in the Philippines, serving Filipino pastries and coffee. Its cozy atmosphere quickly made it a favorite among local artists, writers, and students. Over the years, the cafe has evolved and recently launched a new branch in Toshima, Japan (2024), becoming a hub for creative minds and a spot where revolutionary ideas flourish.
